About St. Fictional's

Hello and welcome to the official website of St. Fictional's National School. We are a ten teacher school located ten minutes from the beautiful Blarney Castle in Co. Cork. The school was built in 1945. We provide the best teachers and facilities for our students. We encourage learning through play and have an inclusive and co-operative culture. Our approach involves working closely with the local community, involving parents in the education of our students. This ensures a high level of collaboration, bringing forward the best ideas for the development and running of our school

We love nature and encourage a love of nature in our students. We have a beautiful school garden which is maintained by both teachers and students. We grow vegetables and flowers and encourage our students to safeguard the environment. We have fair trade school status and are working towards our 5th Green Flag.

We also love to keep active and take part in local and national sporting events. We have achieved great success in national Soccer leagues over the years.
