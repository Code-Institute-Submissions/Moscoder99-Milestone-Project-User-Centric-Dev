About St. Ficational's

St. Fictional's school is located in Faketown, in the heart of LaLaland. We provide the best teachers and facilities for our students. We encourage learning through play and have an inclusive and co-operative culture. Our approach involves working closely with the local community, involving parents in the education of our students. This ensures a high level of collaboration, bringing forward the best ideas for the development and running of our school
