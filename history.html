A brief history of St. Ficational's

The following paragraphs catalog the progress of St. Fictional's in the decades since it was established, and demonstrates how it has continued to go from strength to strength;

1968

St. Fictional's established!

In 1968, a local Faketown family kindly donated the land upon which St. Fictional's was built.

The community raised the funds needed to build the school. The initial school building consisted of two rooms, and all classes were held within the two rooms. The staff consisted of two teachers, one of them acting as the school principal.

1995

New building opened!

In 1995, the initial building for St. Fictional was replaced by a new more permanent structure.

After a lengthy period durign which the local community campaigned for funds, a government grant made it possible to establish a more permanent structure for St. Fictional's possible. The enture build took two years to complete. With the local community growing in numbers, the new building enabled increasing numbers of local children to be educated in the new school building.

2011

Playing fields introduced!

In 2011, St. Fictional unveiled a set of new playing fields.

Once again, the community played a part by donating some of the land, enabling the playing fields to become a reality. The new fields will enable the school to compete in national and international sporting events with great success.
