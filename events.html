St. Fictionals, Faketown

A few of the fantastic activities undertaken in 2019!



The Buddy System


A huge thanks to our Parent's Association for establishing and running the Buddy System.
The children have been working together to develop their reading skills and we have seen excellent results!






Summer Sports Day!


Our Summer Sports day was so much fun!
All of the children did amazingly in their preparation and performance on the day. Congratulations to those who won trophies as well as all of those who competed!






Library Update


The modernisation of our library would not have been possible without the hard work of all of our students, teachers and PTA.
The library has undergone a refresh and modernisation of books and digital equipment. This will future proof the library for years to come.






School Extension!


The opening of the new wing of the school in the Spring was a great event!
The fundraising, preparation of plans and building of the extension took five years. It was great to finally see it up and running and benefiting our students.
